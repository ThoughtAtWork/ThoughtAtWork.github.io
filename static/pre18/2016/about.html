ABOUT US

HISTORY IN
THE MAKING

Thought At Work started out as a small Industrial Design conference, but over the years, dedicated students have caused it to expand considerably. Drawing a crowd of over 500 attendees last year, the conference now strives to be informed by all walks of design: Industrial Design, Interactive Design, Illustration, Motion, Graphic Design, 3D Digital Design, Experiential Design, Film and Animation, Fine Art, and more. We believe our efforts will inspire students after us to step up and bridge the gap between classroom and industry, and we hope you’ll be a part of it.

Thought At Work has always aimed to keep the content fresh and interesting to our generation since students know what students want to learn.
