A Design Conference Made

for Students, by Students.

Every 3rd weekend of October at RIT

every year

We bring industry professionals and passionate students together for a long weekend of collaboration, education, and inspiration.

What We Have

At the conference you’ll have the choice of essential workshops, insightful speaker lectures, fun events, 3 tasty (and free) meals, parties, after-parties, and countless opportunities to network. Thought At Work is a chance to get connected to the design world outside your campus or office.

Who Should Come

Designers of all kind : industrial, graphic, motion, 3D, experiential, and interactive designers and developers. We welcome all levels of creatives from any creative field - especially students - to attend Thought at Work.
