All Thought at Work events take place on or just outside the Rochester Institute of Technology’s campus. RIT’s main address is 1 Lomb Memorial Dr, Rochester, NY 14623.

Once You Arrive

Upon arrival, there will be signs directing you to our registration table. Information about the locations of individual activities will be provided in a brochure upon check-in. You will be given a wristband and name tag that will identify you as an attendee. Wristbands must be worn for admittance into lectures and workshops.

Parking

Parking will be available in lots G and H on RIT's campus.

Check out our campus

Hotels, Lodging and Couch Surfing

If you’re travelling from another school, and don’t want to pay money for a hotel, our student team will be happy to let you crash in one of our apartments for a few nights. For more info on couch-surfing, contact hello@thoughtatwork.org.
