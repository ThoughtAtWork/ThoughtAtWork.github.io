Interested in Sponsoring?

Being a sponsor for Thought at Work is a great way to increase your company’s visibility among a new generation of design students. As Thought at Work is entirely student-run, we thrive on donations from the generosity of people such as yourself.

We offer sponsorship opportunities for all budget levels, including visible booths by our registration desk, advertisements through our social media, and/or your company’s name on Thought at Work marketing materials.

Your donations would assist us in hosting and providing for prolific speakers from various locations, as well as procuring workshop supplies and promotional print media. We understand if you would like your donation earmarked towards specific expenses, to tailor the Thought at Work experience to your brand’s needs.

All donations will be tax-exempt. For more information, contact us.
