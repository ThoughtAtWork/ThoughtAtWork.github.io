Educating Industrial Designers for the Digital Age

Sunand Battacharya | 1:20pm - 1:50pm | Ingle Auditorium

With the advent of new ways of visualizing ideas, developing design solutions, and rapidly prototyping using digital technologies, traditional methods of teaching industrial design in the classroom environment are lately being questioned by educators, employers, and students. The need for successful integration of digital technologies into industrial design education curricula is important for bridging the gap between academic outcomes and industry expectations.
