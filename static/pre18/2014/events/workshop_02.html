Fidelity with Intent

Smart Design | 4:00pm-5:00pm | Booth 1400

From sketches to final specs, a typical project generates artifacts of all levels of refinement. Get practical advice from two seasoned designers about the challenges and benefits of employing the right technique and at the right time. Accompanied by examples from industrial and interaction design, the topic is relevant to any problem solving endeavor.

Designing with Compassion

Rachael Diesel | 5:05pm-6:05pm | Booth 1560

We've all learned how to have empathy for the end users of our products and experiences, but should it stop there? Learn how to incorporate design thinking and an attitude of compassion for your teammates, clients and most of all yourself to avoid the burnout, blowups and bad vibes that keep your end product from being all it can be.

Hack Your Colors

Miguel Cardona | 5:05pm-6:05pm | Booth 1420

Color plays an important role in design, yet color selection in our digital toolboxes leave much to be desired. Basic color theory is often taught without real world application or insight. Miguel will discuss, demystify and breakdown ways to improve color palate selection as well as introduce some tips, hacks, improved models and dynamic tools to more effectively work with on screen color.
