Industrial and Interaction Student Design Conference

Thought at Work is an industrial and interaction design student conference to be held Oct 18-20 2013 at Rochester Institute of Technology. Thought at Work will provide various presentations, workshops, panel discussions a portfolio review and a drawing competition for design students.

Mission


Provide graduate and undergraduate students in the Northeast an opportunity to network with their peers and professionals while educating and provoking discussion about current design topics.

History: 2012 Conference


Our 2012 Conference had over 280 participants from eight schools, and eighteen presenters. Combining lectures, panel discussions, workshops, portfolio reviews, design galleries, and more to inspire young designers.

Our 2012 Sponsors included: Rochester Institute of Technology, Coroflot, SUIID, IDSA, RIT Student Government, Raymond Corp., Moo.com, and Keyshot.
